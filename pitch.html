How it works

01

Intake

Anyone at SEB can submit an idea — through a structured GitHub Issue form. Pitch recordings from hackathons get processed automatically.

  • Structured form: problem, hypothesis, business value, domain
  • Links to pitch video, repo, docs — added anytime
  • MP4 pitch recordings transcribed with AI
  • Contact person always visible — ideas have owners

02

Marketplace

A clean, browsable site where all processed ideas are presented as cards — filterable by domain, status, tech pattern, and more.

  • Two reading modes: Business view and Engineering view
  • Filter by domain, arch pattern, status, score
  • Cluster view — ideas grouped by theme
  • Enabler detection — shared components across ideas

Design principles

⬡

No infrastructure in phase 1

GitHub Issues as intake. Local pipeline. GitHub Pages as output. Zero hosting cost, zero GCP dependency.

⬡

Ideas have owners

Every idea shows who submitted it and how to reach them. Good ideas should have a face.

⬡

Built for two audiences

Business view for C-level and domain leads. Engineering view for architects and developers. Same card, two lenses.
